$MMD(Xs,Xt)=||\frac{1}{ns^2}\sum_i \sum_{i'}k(x^s_i,x^s_{i'})-\frac{1}{nsnt}\sum_i \sum_{j}k(x^s_i,x^t_{j})+\frac{1}{nt^2}\sum_j \sum_{j'}k(x^t_j,x^t_{j'})||_H$

k: 高斯核$k(u,v)=e^{-\frac{||u-v||^2}{\sigma}}$
